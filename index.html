Who I Am

I was born in Kaohsiung, south of Taiwan, in 1980 and have studied for a master of business administration until 2005 at the university of Applied Sciences in Würzburg/Schweinfurt, Germany. Now I work as a housewife and a hobby painter.
我出生於1980，來自南台灣的高雄，最高學歷為德國幅茲堡應用科技大學學習企業管理碩士，目前從事家管跟業餘畫家。
